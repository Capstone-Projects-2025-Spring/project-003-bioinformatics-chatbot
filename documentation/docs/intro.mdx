# Introduction


Are you tired of digging through countless research papers, only to still not find the answer you're looking for? 
The Bioinformatics Chatbot is here to ease your concerns. The Chatbot is your new and personal lab assistant designed to answer 
complicated Bioinformatics problems effortlessly. Built-in React, we use powerful RAG and innovative learning technology to provide
high-quality answers. So upload those long research papers, ask your most challenging questions, and let the Bioinformatics Chatbot turn hours of reading into seconds of clarity.
